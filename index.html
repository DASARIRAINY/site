About Prime Cable Industries

Prime Cable Industries is one of the leading cable manufacturing companies in India since past 21 years


Prime Cable Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a renowned name in manufacturing power and communication cables

We maintain world-class quality since its inception. We are accredited with ISO 9001: 2015 and all our products are also ISI mark certified.

We provide a wide range of cabling solutions to almost all the sectors of the economy including power, telecommunication and information technology, industrial sector, and railways. Our manufacturing unit is equipped with all modern machine and laboratory equipment.

Our vast product catalogue includes aerial-bunch cables, control and instrumentation cables, power cables, high tension power cables, low tension power cables, thermocouple extension and compensating cables, battery cables, and energy cables.
